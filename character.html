Character concept

Meet Milo, the cheeky math monkey.

Milo is a mischievous little guide for the browser game. He reaches for rewards, celebrates correct answers, and reacts with a bundle of goofy sound effects.

Animation approach

Use a layered SVG puppet with tiny transform loops: idle bobbing, tail swishes, eye blinks, and quick reach/celebration poses triggered by gameplay events. It stays lightweight, expressive, and easy to tune for a browser game.
